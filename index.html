About The Weekend

The Fun Begins In

The Bay Area is bringing it back to the Baysics. Join us for a fun filled time in the Bay Area for a weekend to rememeber!

The weekend kicks off with a Meet & Greet held on Friday night!

SATURDAY, will be the Bay Area Bayball tournament. A day of our brothers showing their skills on the court. Ladies, you are more than welcome to attend. Food will be served for all registered players & concessions will also be available.

Following the tournamnet, the official Bayball After-Party will be held in Cali's very own San Francisco.

And then comes an evening of elegance. Join us in the East Bay for Baysic's Black & White Affair. This formal event will be held on a yacht which will cruise the San Francisco Bay!
